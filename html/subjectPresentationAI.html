Artificial Intelligence

ECTS: 6

Artificial Intelligence (AI) introduces fundamental concepts, applications, and its role on the web. The course covers main algorithms and emphasizes Python's role in AI, including libraries like NumPy, SciPy, and sklearn for data and text mining. Students learn supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning, exploring techniques such as Support Vector Machines, neural networks, and deep learning. Additionally, the curriculum covers virtual assistants, natural language processing, rule-based systems, and chatbot methodologies.
