ESMAD (School of Media Arts and Design)

The Course Emphasizes:

Dynamic Learning
Environment

Students have access to resources to support their education. The course is designed to meet industry standards and emerging trends in technology.

Practical Learning

With opportunities for students to work on real-world projects, internships, and collaborations with industry partners. This hands-on experience allows students to apply their knowledge in practical settings and develop the skills and competencies needed to succeed in the field.

Programming Languages

Students learn various programming languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, SQL, and others, and develop skills to create dynamic websites, web applications, and robust information systems.
