Application Interfaces and Design

ECTS: 6

Application Interfaces and Design are crucial for TSIW students as they learn to create effective graphical elements and user-centered designs across various platforms. This course covers essential principles of User Experience (UX) and interface design, focusing on navigation, information architecture, and usability. Students develop skills in synthesizing ideas into visual metaphors and crafting cohesive visual identities, using methodologies like Wireframing. This prepares them to design intuitive and impactful digital interfaces.
